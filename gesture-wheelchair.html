Gesture Controlled Wheelchair

  • Hand gesture recognition using accelerometer and gyroscope sensors.
  • Arduino-based controller translates gestures into motor commands.
  • Ultrasonic sensors for obstacle detection and collision prevention.
  • Emergency stop function and speed regulation for safety.
  • Improves accessibility for users with limited motor control.
